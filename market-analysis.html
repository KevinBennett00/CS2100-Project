Common Trading Terms

What is Volatility?

Volatility refers to how much the price of an asset moves up or down in a given period. High volatility means large price swings.

What is Liquidity?

Liquidity describes how quickly and easily an asset can be bought or sold without significantly affecting its price.

What is a Trading Pair?

A trading pair refers to two currencies that are being traded against each other (e.g., BTC/USDT).
